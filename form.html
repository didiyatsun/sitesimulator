On this page you will find an explanation of the following items.

  • header
  • nav
  • section
  • article
  • aside
  • footer

Header - a header is a "site header", usually it contains the most important information for site visitors.

Nav - nav is intended for navigational links, that is, for those that lead to another section or place on the site.

Section - an element that includes sections.

Article - should exist on its own independently of the rest of the site.

Aside - defines, for example, a sidebar.

Footer - bottom bar of the site with important information.
